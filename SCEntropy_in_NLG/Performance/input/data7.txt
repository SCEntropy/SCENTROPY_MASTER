### REFERENCE ###
Integrate consent mechanisms that give users control over their personal data, allowing them to optin or optout of data sharing as they see fit.

### UNCONSTRAINED ###
Establish user-governed data permissions enabling individuals to selectively authorize or decline information distribution.

### CONSTRAINED ###
To effectively integrate consent mechanisms, it is essential to provide users with full control over their personal data by enabling them to choose whether to opt in or opt out of data sharing based on their preferences.
