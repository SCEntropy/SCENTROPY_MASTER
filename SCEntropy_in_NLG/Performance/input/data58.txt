### REFERENCE ###
Interpret the confidence interval as a range within which the true population mean is likely to fall with the specified confidence level.

### UNCONSTRAINED ###
Regard the confidence interval as the probable span where the actual average of the entire group resides, given the chosen degree of certainty.

### CONSTRAINED ###
The confidence interval represents a range of values within which the true population mean is expected to lie, given the chosen confidence level, indicating the reliability of the estimate.
