### REFERENCE ###
Biocompatibility testing: Implementing rigorous biocompatibility testing protocols to assess and validate the immunogenicity of 3D bioprinted organs before clinical use.

### UNCONSTRAINED ###
Prior to clinical application, 3D-printed organs must undergo stringent assessments to verify their immunological safety through established biocompatibility evaluation procedures.

### CONSTRAINED ###
Establishing comprehensive biocompatibility assessments to rigorously examine and confirm the immunological safety of bioprinted organ constructs prior to their introduction into clinical applications.
